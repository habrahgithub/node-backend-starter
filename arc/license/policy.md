# ARC License Compliance Policy

## Allowed Licenses
- MIT
- Apache-2.0
- BSD-3-Clause
- ISC

## Restricted Licenses
- AGPL (all versions)
- GPL (all versions, without explicit Prime approval)
- Proprietary licenses without a formal agreement.
- Unlicensed code.

## Dependency Verification Procedure
- All dependencies must be scanned for license information before integration.
- A Software Bill of Materials (SBOM) must be generated for every build.

## Attribution Requirements
- All required license attributions must be collected and included in the final product distribution.

## License Audit Process
- Regular license audits will be performed by Sentinel.
