# Platform Specs (Baseline)

## Instagram

- Feed portrait: 1080x1350 (4:5)
- Feed square: 1080x1080 (1:1)
- Story/Reel: 1080x1920 (9:16)

## TikTok

- Vertical video: 1080x1920 (9:16)

## LinkedIn

- Image post: 1200x1200 (1:1) or 1080x1350 (4:5)
- Video: 1080x1920 (9:16) or 1920x1080 (16:9)

## X/Twitter

- Image: 1600x900 (16:9) or 1080x1080 (1:1)

## Safe-Zone Rule

Keep key text/logo away from outer 10% edges for mobile UI overlays.
