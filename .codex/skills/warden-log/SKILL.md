# Warden Log

## Workflow

1. Record task intent and scope at session start.
2. Log executed commands and high-value outcomes.
3. Record decisions and rationale when plans change.
4. Track failures, mitigations, and unresolved follow-ups.
5. End with a concise status snapshot and next actions.

## Logging Rules

- Keep entries factual and timestamped.
- Avoid dumping raw logs; summarize with evidence paths.
- Highlight blockers and ownership clearly.

## Output Rules

- Include what changed, what passed, what failed, and what remains.
- Keep log structure consistent across entries.
